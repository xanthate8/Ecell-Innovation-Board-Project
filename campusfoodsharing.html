The Food Waste Crisis

Universities waste 22 million pounds of edible food annually while 1 in 3 students face food insecurity. Perfectly good meals end up in landfills.

Our Sharing Solution

A campus network where dining halls, events, and students can share surplus food with those who need it, all through our easy-to-use platform.

Why SharePlate Works

Impact Tracking

How SharePlate Works

1. List Surplus Food

Dining halls, events, or students snap a photo of available food and set a pickup window

2. Claim & Collect

Students browse nearby listings and reserve portions through our app

3. Enjoy & Connect

Recipients pick up meals and donors get recognition for their contributions
