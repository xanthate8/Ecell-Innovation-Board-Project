How it Works?

1. Connect Your Calendar

Links with Google Calendar, Outlook, or university systems. Automatically imports all classes, deadlines, and events

2. Receive Smart Suggestions

Analyzes your workload to create optimal study blocks. Suggests meeting times that work for all group members. Alerts you about schedule conflicts before they happen

3. Navigate & Execute

AR-powered directions to classrooms/labs. Real-time updates about room changes or closures. Breaks projects into daily steps. Sends reminders to your phone/email.
